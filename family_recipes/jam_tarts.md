# Jam tarts

## Ingredients

- 6 oz Cake flour
- 2 oz Potato flour
- 4 oz Castor sugar
- 6 oz Margarine
- 1 Egg, beaten

## Method

1. Mix cake flour, potato flour and sugar in a bowl
2. Rub in marge ( or butter )
3. Add beaten egg
4. Bake at 180 C for 15-20 minutes

## Notes

- Makes 3 and a half dozen/ 6 dozen
